Expressions are used by directives like `set_header`, `set_query`, and `model_map_default`.

## Important gotcha

Built-in variables are expanded only when used as bare expressions:

- ✅ `set_query stream $request.stream;`
- ❌ `set_query stream "$request.stream";` (becomes a literal string)

See the upstream `DSL_SYNTAX.md` for the complete list of built-in variables and functions.
